# Ecommerce-Sales-Dashboard

**Overview**
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of ecommerce sales performance across multiple dimensions — time, geography, customer behavior, and product categories. Designed for business stakeholders, it enables quick insights into revenue, profitability, and purchasing trends.

**Key Features**

•	**KPI Panel:**

Total Sales Amount: ₹438K
Total Profit: ₹37K
Average Order Value (AOV): ₹121K
Total Quantity Sold: 5615

•	**Time Analysis:**

Monthly profit trends visualized with dynamic bar charts
Quarter-based filtering for seasonal performance review

•	**Geographic Insights:**

Top-performing states (e.g., Maharashtra, Delhi)
Regional breakdown of sub-category sales

•	**Customer & Product Segmentation:**

Top 4 customers by revenue
Top 5 sub-categories by sales volume
Category distribution: Clothing, Electronics, Furniture

•	**Payment Behavior:**

Mode of payment analysis: COD, UPI, Debit, Credit

**Business Impact**

This dashboard helps ecommerce managers and analysts:
•	Identify high-performing regions and products
•	Monitor profitability and order value trends
•	Understand customer preferences and payment modes
•	Make data-driven decisions for marketing and inventory planning

**Tools & Skills Used**

•	Power BI: Data modeling, DAX measures, slicers, and interactive visuals
•	Data Visualization: Color theory, layout planning, and KPI storytelling
•	Business Analytics: Sales performance analysis and customer segmentation
